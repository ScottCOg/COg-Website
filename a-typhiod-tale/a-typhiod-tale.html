Meet the Team!

COg. Projects are a collaborative effort and the teams are developed by volunteers working to find the solution.

Dr. Dena Ferguson

Dena Ferguson is a general surgeon specializing in global surgery. In her work in East and West Africa, she treated so much typhoid and saw so many preventable deaths that she resolved to increase awareness and appropriate treatment of typhoid. She is the Idea Generator of A Typhoid Tale.

CUT THE MUSTARD

Cut The Mustard is a UK based Animation Studio helping us out with the A Typhoid Tale's Animation and Production

LUCKY HALF SPEED

Lucky Half Speed is a Nashville, Tennessee based creative group helping us out with the A Typhoid Tale's audio creation and engineering
